what is checkin?

CheckIn is a service that utilizes three main technologies: a smartphone, a website, and bluetooth beacons. The idea is that we can make smartphones smarter by using small bluetooth beacons that send out signals. We take those signals and use them to allow your customers to CheckIn so you can see who's at your place, and who isn't. It's convenient for medical practices, hospitals, concerts, parties, and all kinds of other events!

There's a few things though that you'll need and have to do first, party animals, before you can go crazy.

  1. You need a low energy bluetooth beacon. We recommend Estimote!
  2. Register on our website as an establishment.
  3. Put your beacon near a place where all of your users can CheckIn.

And that's it! Easy, huh?

how we started

CheckIn started off as a competition project for the 2014 Ohio State Hackathon where it won first prize because everyone thought it was super awesome. We like to think it's because of the time, love and care we put in to making this great service available to the world! Thirty-six hours spent in the basement of a library spawned the easy check-in service you have come to know and love. It was made with lots of pizza, coffee, and the occasional Key & Peele video to get us through.

We are committed towards continually improving our service and we strive to give you guys, our loyal users, the best and easiest experience possible.

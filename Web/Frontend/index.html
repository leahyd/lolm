welcome!

Welcome to CheckIn, the easiest way to check-in! If you have a registered beacon, then this site is where you'll come to see who's checked in to your establishment. If you're new, please follow the instructions on the Register page. If you have already registered your establishment with us, then go ahead and login in!

On this site, you'll be given some easy-to-use tools to view customer data (like who has checked in), create forms for your customers to fill out on the CheckIn App, communicate with your customers, and more. It's your one stop shop for everything you'll need to manage your CheckIn system!

Below you'll find a short video on Estimote, the bluetooth beacon technology that we encourage you to work with! In the video, you'll find out what the beacon can do and hopefully learn a little bit about how we check you in.

Welcome to LoL Mastery Battle where you can battle your friends for mastery and for glory! Sure, you may kick ass on the Rift, but when the nexus is in ruins and the LolClient is closed, this is where you come to show off your mad skills?

Register your LoL account and pick a set of champions to field. Then, simply challenge friends or a random opponent to see who is truly the master of their champion. A challenge puts you in a series of games where your mastery score is tallied. Simply attain the higher mastery score in your series than your opponent with your selected champion and you win!
